Wedding Details

If you’re here, you’re supposed to be here. This page has the main wedding details, a few helpful notes, and the answers to the questions people were probably going to ask us anyway.

The Ceremony

This is the part where we actually get married. It’ll be short, meaningful, and then we’ll head over to Pavilion 1 for food and Pavilion 1B for everything else.

Location

The wedding will be held at Lums Pond State Park at Pavilion 1. Once you’re in the park, just follow the signs and you’ll find us.

Daily entry is $5 for Delaware vehicles and $10 for out-of-state — so… carpool if you love your wallet.

The Day’s Flow

When you arrive, you’ll grab your welcome bag at the entrance, then we’ll have the ceremony. After that, we’ll all head over to Pavilion 1 for food, and Pavilion 1B will be open for activities and hanging out for the rest of the day.

Food & Drinks

We’ll have BBQ, food, and drinks ready to go. It’s all casual, so feel free to grab what you want and go back for more.

Good to Know

This is an outdoor park wedding, so expect grass, weather, and a relaxed setup. You’ll be at picnic tables for most of the day, and late September weather can be… unpredictable.
